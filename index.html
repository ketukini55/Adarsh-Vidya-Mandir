Our Journey

Six Decades of Excellence

From humble beginnings to a thriving centre of learning — our story is one of passion, community, and endless dedication.

1963

School Founded

Adarsh Vidya Mandir established under Nutan Vidya Vikas Mandal, beginning its mission to bring quality education to the children of Kelve.

🌱
🏫
1980s

Growing Community

The school's reputation grew across Palghar district as enrolment surged and new classrooms were built to welcome more eager young learners.

2000s

Cultural Recognition

Students began winning district and state-level competitions in arts, sports, and academics — putting AVM firmly on the map of excellence.

🏆
📱
2015+

Digital Transformation

Smart interactive boards introduced in classrooms, bringing digital learning and 21st-century education tools to our students.

Today

Bright Future Ahead

With 500+ students, 25+ dedicated teachers, and a tradition of excellence, AVM continues to shine brighter than ever — lighting the path ahead.
